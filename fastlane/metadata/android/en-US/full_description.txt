FOSStenbuch is a free and open source digital trip logbook for Android – designed for individuals, freelancers, and businesses who want to document their trips easily, privately, and without any cloud dependency.

Features:

* Trip recording – Create trips with start/end locations, odometer readings, purpose, and vehicle
* GPS tracking – Background location tracking via foreground service
* Vehicle management – Manage multiple vehicles
* Trip purposes – Define custom purposes (business, private, etc.)
* Saved locations – Save and reuse frequently visited places
* Odometer tracking – Seamless documentation of odometer readings
* Statistics – Evaluations and overviews of trips and kilometers
* Export – Export trips as PDF or CSV and share them
* Backup & Restore – Local data backup and recovery
* Reminders – Notifications for trip recording
* Audit log – Traceable change history of trips
* Templates – Save recurring trips as templates
* Material Design – Modern Android UI with Material 3 components
* Offline-first – All data is stored locally on the device (Room database)
* No tracking, no ads, no cloud – Full data control
